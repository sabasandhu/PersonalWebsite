About ME

My name is Saba Sandhu and I am a senior at the University of Oklahoma studying Accounting, Management Information Systems, and Spanish with an expected graduation date in May 2021. As a student at the Price College of Business, I have been given the opportunity to be a part of multiple organizations on campus like the highly selective JCPenney Leadership Program and the Women in Business Association. JCPenney is a highly competitive program that selects only the top 3% of business students and helps ensure career success by building professional leaders. Being a part of the Women in Business Association has also allowed me to find a community of strong women who are passionate about progressing their professionalism as well as empowering other women.
